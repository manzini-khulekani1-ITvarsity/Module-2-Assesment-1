RHINO-BEETLE

Dynastinae or rhinoceros beetles are a subfamily of the scarab beetle family.

Other common names – some for particular groups of rhinoceros beetles – include Hercules beetles, unicorn beetles or horn beetles.

Over 1500 species and 225 genera of rhinoceros beetles are known.
